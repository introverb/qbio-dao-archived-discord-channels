@everyone The Quantum Biology Ecosystem is hosting a Mascot Design Contest to create a visual identity for our field. The mascot will be used across our platforms to represent quantum biology—capturing its scientific depth without relying on mystical or abstract imagery. The Details:
  • Who can enter? Anyone (+AI-assisted designs are allowed).
  • What’s the theme? The mascot should represent the full scope of quantum biology. Think molecular interactions, superposition, magnetic fields, biological processes at the quantum level, and more.
  • How do I submit? Post up to three designs in this channel.
  • What happens to my design? All submissions will be public domain (CC license) so they can be freely used by the community.
  • How is the winner chosen? Voting will take place on March 30th.
Winner gets $50, and five runner-ups get $10. Deadline: Friday, March 28 at 11:59pm PST. Join us in shaping the future of quantum biology!

[Quantum Biology] Open position for cell or molecular biologist at the Quantum Biology Institute Dear Quantum Biology community, On the heels of our recent paper on magnetic field effects in biology, the Quantum Biology Institute is looking for a full-time cell or molecular biologist to join the team. The candidate should be Ph.D.-level and be able to work out of our Los Angeles laboratory. This position offers at least a three-year contract with a start date during the first trimester of 2025. The Institute is a focused research organization that over the next 3–5 years will build novel instruments to unambiguously prove or refute the Quantum Biology Hypothesis, which is that quantum states last for long enough inside cells to be biologically relevant. As part of the project, we'll need the insight of a skilled biologist able to work well in a fast moving and self reliant culture. If you are interested in please send your CV and a brief expression of interest to clarice@quantumbiology.org. Warm regards, Clarice D.

Hi@everyone HAPPY WEEKEND! *MARKET MAKERS(MM) *Let’s kick off the weekend with some crypto knowledge. Today, lets talk about Market Makers an important part of the crypto world. Have you ever wondered why your buy or sell orders get filled instantly even when a token isn’t trending or heavily traded? That’s where market makers come in. Market makers provide the liquidity needed to keep the market flowing. They help ensure that any time you place a trade, there’s someone on the other side to fulfill it. This keeps the market stable and efficient, even in low activity times. HOW MARKET MAKERS WORKS; --On Centralized Exchanges (CEX) like Binance or KuCoin, market makers are visible in the order book. They continuously place buy and sell orders to match incoming trades. --On Decentralized Exchanges (DEX) like Uniswap or PancakeSwap, there is no order book. Instead, market makers provide liquidity to liquidity pools. When you trade, tokens are taken from or added to the pool enabling smooth swaps. So whether you're buying or selling, your trade is made possible by the liquidity that market makers provide. Next time your order goes through instantly, thank the market makers they are always working in the background. why was the market makers important,Do they have any positive or negative impact on token price? **ETHEREUM FOUNDATION Unveils New Treasury Strategy to Secure Its ETH Reserves The Treasury Strategy Defipunk Philosophy The Ethereum Foundation (EF) has rolled out a new treasury strategy that aims to protect its ETH reserves with a 2.5-year runway while also targeting a 5% spending limit. The new plan prioritizes transparency, operational efficiency, and alignment with the blockchain’s long-standing values. The Treasury Strategy In a June 4 blog post, the Foundation outlined plans to allocate 15% of its treasury annually toward operational expenses, while maintaining a financial buffer sufficient to cover two and a half years of spending. Under this model, ETH will only be sold if the liquid reserves dip below this threshold. Reserve levels will be assessed quarterly to determine whether asset sales are necessary, helping preserve treasury strength amid market fluctuations. “We intend to reduce annual opex roughly linearly over the next five years, ending at a long-term 5% baseline that is common for endowment-based organizations,” the post read. It explained that the policy reflects the organization’s belief that 2025-26 is likely to be pivotal for Ethereum, requiring focused investment in key outcomes. “We will frequently reallocate funds between protocols for reasons such as changing market conditions, diversification, or new yield opportunities,” the organization wrote. It emphasized that withdrawals should be understood in that context and not interpreted as anti-endorsements. The non-profit also wants to grow and manage its treasury using secure, permissionless, and audited DeFi protocols. This will include independently staking ETH and lending wrapped ETH (wETH) through established DeFi platforms. Further, it plans to diversify its portfolio periodically by converting some assets into fiat currency.
